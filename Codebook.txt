#codebook for the features

meanX = mean of the measurements of acceleration of body in x direction for each activity_label and each subject
  ; same applies to meanY and meanZ.
  
stdX = mean of the measurements of standard deviations of body in X direction for each activity_label and each
 Subject; same applies to stdY and stdZ.
 
 Activity_labels = each subject performs 6 activities labeled as WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS,
                  STANDING, SITTING, LAYING.

Subject = there are 30 subjects who were employed to get the measurements.
